About FreshBites

FreshBites is the official campus canteen management system designed to provide students and staff with a seamless dining experience. Our mission is to serve fresh, nutritious, and delicious meals while reducing wait times through our innovative online ordering system.

Established in 2023, we've served over 50,000 meals to our campus community with a focus on quality, affordability, and convenience.
